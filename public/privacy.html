Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nozzle Nest (“we”, “us”) collects and uses personal information when you visit our website and purchase our products.

Who we are

Nozzle Nest is a small business based in the UK. If you have any questions about this policy or your personal data, contact: simon@nozzle-nest.com.

What data we collect

  • Order and contact details such as your name, email address, delivery address, and the items you purchase.
  • Payment processing details are handled by Stripe. We do not store your card details.
  • Website usage data such as basic information about how you use the site (for example page visits), if analytics are enabled.

How we use your data

  • To process and fulfil your order (including delivery).
  • To provide customer support and handle returns/refunds.
  • To meet legal, accounting, and tax obligations.
  • If you contact us, to respond to your enquiry.

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. When you check out, you are redirected to Stripe’s secure payment pages. Stripe acts as a payment processor and handles payment information in accordance with its own privacy policy.

Sharing your data

We only share personal information where necessary to run the business, including:

  • Stripe to process payments.
  • Delivery services to ship your order.
  • Professional advisers (e.g., accountants) where required.

Legal basis

We process personal data where it is necessary to perform a contract (fulfil your order), comply with legal obligations, and for legitimate interests (running and improving the business), where those interests are not overridden by your rights.

How long we keep your data

We keep order records for as long as needed for customer support and to meet legal and tax requirements. We do not keep card details.

Your rights

You have rights under UK data protection law, including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances. To exercise your rights, contact us at simon@nozzle-nest.com.

Cookies

Our site may use essential cookies and, if enabled, analytics cookies to help us understand website usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about privacy, please email simon@nozzle-nest.com.
